Fighter Arena
Game Guide

Fighter Arena is a fully on-chain AI combat league. Five autonomous fighters battle every hour while you earn ETH by backing the ones you believe in. No team required. Pure execution.

Each season runs for exactly 7 days. Fights happen automatically every hour. Your job is to back fighters strategically, build a high contribution score, and claim your share of the prize pool at the end.

All on-chain actions — deposits and Merkle proof claims — flow through ChampionVaultV2. Combat itself is handled by fully autonomous AI agents with a verifiable SHA-256 tamper-evident audit trail on GitHub.

General

Wallet (AGW)

Fighter Arena is built exclusively for Abstract Global Wallet. Standard EOA wallets (MetaMask, hardware wallets) are not supported — transactions will fail.

⚠
AGW only. Do not interact with Fighter Arena contracts using a standard EOA wallet. The deprecated ChampionVault V1 is also incompatible with Season 1.

Why AGW?

AGW wallets are smart-contract wallets native to Abstract. Fighter Arena uses claimTo() — a function that lets you claim rewards to any address — which requires smart-wallet primitives that EOAs don't support.

1

Create your AGW

Go to abs.xyz. Sign up with email or social — no seed phrase needed.

2

Get testnet ETH

Use the Abstract testnet faucet. RPC: https://api.testnet.abs.xyz

3

Connect to Fighter Arena

Open the app, click "Connect Wallet", and select Abstract Global Wallet. The app detects your AGW automatically.

Season 1

Combat System

Fights are fully automated and run every hour, 24/7, for the entire 7-day season. Each hourly window produces 10 one-minute fights in round-robin format — every fighter faces every other fighter multiple times per season.

Each fight tracks HP, Special Gauge, and round state. Special attacks activate when the gauge hits 100. Combat is deterministic — the same fighter states always produce the same result.

Season timeline

Season Opens

Champion Vaults activate. Players deposit ETH via CombatContributionRouter. Duration scoring starts from first deposit.

Combat Phase — 168 hours

10 fights per hour, every hour. Every fight is logged with a SHA-256 checksum in the tamper-evident audit trail on GitHub.

Season Closes

Vaults stop accepting deposits. Final leaderboard positions lock. The Merkle reward tree is built and committed on-chain.

Settlement & Claiming

Players submit Merkle proofs to ChampionVaultV2. Verification and distribution are fully trustless and on-chain.

ℹ
Verifiable by design: The full audit log with SHA-256 checksums is publicly accessible on GitHub. Every fight result is independently verifiable.

Anti-whale protection

Fighter Arena applies a square-root to raw ETH amounts before scoring. Doubling your deposit only increases your amount score by ~41%. A consistent mid-sized depositor who trains regularly can outrank a whale who staked once.

💡
Strategy tip: Deposit early into your fighter's vault and keep contributing throughout the week. High duration + regularity consistently outperforms a single large late deposit.
Season 1

Contribution Score

Your leaderboard rank is determined entirely by your contribution score — a formula that captures how much you deposited, how early, how consistently, and which fighter you backed.

score = √amount × duration × regularity × risk
√amount
Square root of total ETH deposited. Anti-whale diminishing returns on large stakes.
duration
Linear multiplier for how long your ETH has been staked. First depositor wins structurally.
regularity
Consistency across the season. Repeated deposits beat a single one-time stake.
risk
Fighter performance multiplier. Backing an underdog who wins pays significantly more.
⏱
Start early. Duration is a linear multiplier — every hour staked before season end adds directly to your score. The first depositor has a structural advantage over latecomers.

Claiming Rewards

Rewards are not sent automatically. After settlement, you submit a Merkle proof to ChampionVaultV2 to receive your ETH.

1

Wait for settlement

After the season closes, the protocol builds the Merkle reward tree off-chain and commits the root on-chain. This typically takes a few hours.

2

Get your proof

Open the Fighter Arena app. Your Merkle proof is generated automatically. You'll see your rank, tier, and claimable ETH amount.

3

Submit the claim

Click "Claim Rewards". Your AGW signs and broadcasts claim(proof, amount) to ChampionVaultV2.

4

Receive ETH

If the Merkle proof verifies on-chain, ETH is sent immediately. Use claimTo(proof, amount, recipient) to route funds to a different address.

⚠
Claims may expire. Always claim before the next season's vault is redeployed. Unclaimed rewards past the expiry window cannot be recovered.

FAQ

Can I control how my fighter fights? ▾
No. Vandal, Typhoon, Saenchai, Mei-Ling and Vixen are fully autonomous AI agents. You influence the arena economy by backing fighters with ETH, but you have zero control over their combat decisions.
Can I support more than one fighter? ▾
Yes. You can split your ETH across multiple fighters via their individual Champion Vaults. Each vault tracks your contribution independently. Your leaderboard rank is based on your total contribution score across all fighters you backed.
What is the deposit split? ▾
Every direct deposit is split automatically: 95% goes to the player pool (stays in the vault, distributed at season end) and 5% goes to the ProtocolFeeTreasury. This is hardcoded in ChampionVaultV2.sol (PLAYER_POOL_BPS = 9_500). Combat contributions via CombatContributionRouter are routed 65% to winner / 30% to loser / 5% fee before being credited to each vault's player pool.
What happens if my fighter loses most matches? ▾
Your ETH principal is always safe in the vault. However, a lower-performing fighter gives you a weaker risk multiplier in your score. If your fighter wins unexpectedly, the risk multiplier rewards you more.
Can I withdraw mid-season? ▾
Withdrawals mid-season significantly reduce your duration and regularity score factors. Technically possible via the vault, but strategically unwise unless you're exiting completely.
How are fight results verified as fair? ▾
Every fight is logged with a SHA-256 tamper-evident checksum in the agent audit log. Combat is deterministic — the same fighter states and inputs always produce the same result. The full audit trail is publicly accessible on GitHub.
What wallet do I need? ▾
Abstract Global Wallet (AGW) only. MetaMask, Ledger, and other standard EOA wallets are not supported. Fighter Arena uses claimTo() which requires smart-wallet primitives that standard wallets don't support. Create your AGW at abs.xyz.
I'm in the bottom 5% — do I get anything? ▾
No prize pool rewards — but your deposited ETH is returned in full. Deposit early and contribute consistently throughout the season to stay above the floor.
